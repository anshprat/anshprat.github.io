The early morning of June 11th (or June 10 evening for the folks in US west coast) saw wordpress.com going down! This is the first time that I remember such a big outage ever since I started with wordpress. It was just giving an error message saying "Wordpress will be back shortly!". Millions of blogs hosted on wordpress were affected because of this. Also non-wordpress hosted blogs using wordpress were affected for their stats. As the stats plugin uses API keys issued by wordpress.
[caption id="attachment_141" align="alignleft" width="779" caption="Wordpress.com goes down, affects wordpress stats as well."][/caption]

Majority of wordpress blogs are up now and rest are slowly crawling back to normalcy. The main site is yet to be restored.
